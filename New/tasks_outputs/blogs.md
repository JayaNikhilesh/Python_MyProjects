1. **Revenue Impact** – Enterprises report **15‑30 % cost reduction** by automating repetitive decision loops with agents.  
2. **Cross‑Domain Demand** – From **banking (RBI‑compliant agents)** to **health‑care (clinical decision‑support agents)**, every sector needs a specialist.  
3. **Skill Premium** – The **average total compensation** for an AI‑Agents developer in India now exceeds **₹25 LPA**, with senior roles crossing **₹55 LPA**.  
4. **Learning Curve Is Sharper Than Ever** – Frameworks such as **LangChain, Haystack, CrewAI** and **Microsoft Copilot Studio** have **extensive documentation**, making self‑learning feasible within 3‑4 months.
